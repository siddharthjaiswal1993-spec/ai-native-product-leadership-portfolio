## Positioning

I build enterprise software that reasons, not just reports. My work sits at the intersection of workflow automation, AI-native product design, and enterprise governance — building systems that detect risk, diagnose root causes, recommend actions, route approvals, and verify measurable business outcomes.

Nine years in B2B SaaS. Six-plus years in product management. Sprinklr, Accorian, Aquera Labs, Delightree. Now focused on Principal PM and AI Product Leadership roles where the product has to work at enterprise scale and the stakes are real.

---

## Portfolio Thesis

Enterprise software is moving from systems of record to systems of workflow to systems of intelligence. The next generation of AI-native B2B SaaS products will not just summarise information. They will detect risks, diagnose root causes, recommend actions, route approvals, and verify measurable business outcomes.

The PMs who will lead this wave are not those who learned to write ChatGPT prompts. They are the ones who understand workflow design, trust architecture, evaluation methodology, human-in-the-loop governance, and the unit economics of deploying AI at enterprise scale.
