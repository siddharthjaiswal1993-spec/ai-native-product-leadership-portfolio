## What Still Needs Real Proof

### The Honest Gap List

**Shipped product metrics are limited.** Delightree results (2× ARR, 150+ brands, 25K users) are the strongest proof points. The AI portfolio repos are concepts and prototypes. When asked about AI product impact, anchor on Delightree and Aquera; use the portfolio to demonstrate AI product thinking, not shipped AI at scale.

**No live production AI feature.** The portfolio includes agent design, RAG architecture, and eval frameworks — but no deployed AI feature with real usage metrics. The RehabPlan AI and Franchise Ops prototypes are the closest to a working UI. This is the single most important gap to close if moving into a senior AI PM role.

**Case studies lack proprietary customer data.** All case studies use synthetic data. A real shipped product case study — with real before/after metrics, real customer feedback, and a real adoption curve — would be significantly more compelling than the best synthetic case study.

**Eval frameworks are theoretical.** The eval framework documents describe best practices accurately, but they have not been run on real model outputs. Operational credibility here requires either a side project with a real model evaluation or a role where you run evals on a production AI feature.

**No AI red-team or adversarial testing experience documented.** The risk and guardrail documents are thorough, but adversarial testing (prompt injection, jailbreak attempts, data exfiltration vectors) is increasingly table stakes for senior AI PM roles at security-conscious companies.
