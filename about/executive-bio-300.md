# Executive Bio — 300 Words

Siddharth Jaiswal is an AI-native enterprise product leader with nine years in B2B SaaS and six-plus years in product management, specialising in workflow automation, AI agents, platform governance, and operational intelligence.

He began his career at Sprinklr, where he spent five years moving from Lead Product Specialist into product management, building deep fluency in enterprise social infrastructure, multi-stakeholder workflows, and large-scale platform governance. He then joined Accorian as a product manager focused on identity and access management automation, followed by Aquera Labs, where he led the productisation of AI-assisted identity integration — reducing customer time-to-value by 50 percent and integration error rates by 75 percent through AI-generated connector configurations combined with structured human review.

Most recently, Siddharth served as Staff PM and Founding PM at Delightree, where he built the product from early-stage into a franchise operations platform serving 150-plus franchise brands and 25,000 end users, growing ARR 2× during his tenure. His most significant work at Delightree was the design and launch of Delphi 2.0, an AI diagnostic layer that synthesises operational signals across franchise locations, surfaces root cause findings, and routes recommended actions to field consultants and operators — with full human approval before any customer-facing action.

Siddharth's core belief is that enterprise software is moving from systems of record to systems of workflow to systems of intelligence, and that the PMs who will lead this transition are those who understand eval design, trust architecture, HITL governance, and the unit economics of AI at scale — not just model capabilities.

He maintains a public portfolio of ten AI-native product prototypes and is open to product leadership conversations in enterprise B2B SaaS.
