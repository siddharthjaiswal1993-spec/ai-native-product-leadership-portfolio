# Testimonial Request Template

Use this template to request a LinkedIn recommendation. Personalize the [BRACKETED] fields. Keep the email short — give them a suggested paragraph so they can edit rather than write from scratch.

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** Quick ask — LinkedIn recommendation?

Hi [NAME],

I hope you're doing well. I'm reaching out because I'm exploring new opportunities and would love a LinkedIn recommendation from you if you're open to it.

You'd be one of the best people to speak to [SPECIFIC THING — e.g., "my product work on the Delphi 2.0 AI diagnostic layer" / "how I approached the integration assistant product at Aquera" / "the cross-functional coordination work on the platform migration"].

To make it as easy as possible, here's a suggested paragraph you can edit or rewrite however feels right:

---

*Suggested paragraph:*

"I worked with Siddharth [at / alongside] [COMPANY] during [TIME PERIOD]. [One sentence on the context — the project, team, or challenge.] What stood out about Siddharth was [SPECIFIC QUALITY — e.g., his ability to translate ambiguous product requirements into clear technical specs / his instinct for enterprise customer pain / his rigorous thinking about AI product design and governance]. [One specific example or outcome, even brief.] I would recommend him to any team building complex B2B SaaS products, especially in AI-native or platform contexts."

---

Feel free to edit this completely — I just want to make sure it doesn't take too much of your time.

Thank you in advance.

Best,
Siddharth

---

## Notes on Personalisation

- Choose a recommender based on what the target role values: technical depth (choose an engineering peer), customer empathy (choose a CS or sales partner), leadership (choose a manager or executive)
- The suggested paragraph should reflect something specific — a project, a decision, an outcome — not generic praise
- Send no more than 3 of these requests at a time; follow up once after 10 days if no response
- After receiving a recommendation, send a personal thank-you note and offer to return the favour
