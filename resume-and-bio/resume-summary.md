## Formatting Notes

- **Length:** 150 words is the right length for a senior PM resume summary. Recruiters scan; they do not read. Every sentence must carry specific information.
- **No objectives:** Do not open with "I am looking for..." — the summary should be about what you offer, not what you want.
- **Lead with context:** Company name + outcome is the fastest way to establish credibility. "Founding PM at Delightree, where I drove 2× ARR growth" conveys more in 10 words than any adjective-heavy description.
- **Specifics beat adjectives:** "130+ enterprise connectors" beats "strong integration experience." "2× ARR" beats "significant business impact."
- **End with openness:** "Open to principal PM and AI product leadership conversations" is specific, professional, and signals intent without desperation.
