# Executive Bio — 300 Words

Under his leadership, the platform delivered 2× ARR growth in one year.

Siddharth's portfolio spans ten AI-native product concepts and prototypes across franchise operations, product intelligence, customer success, FinOps, CRM adoption, sales readiness, and identity. His work demonstrates depth in agent architecture, RAG system design, AI evaluation frameworks, HITL governance, and responsible AI for enterprise contexts.
