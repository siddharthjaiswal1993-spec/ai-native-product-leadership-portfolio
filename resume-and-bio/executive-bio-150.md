## Bio (~150 Words)

Siddharth Jaiswal is a Principal PM and AI product leader with nine years of experience building enterprise B2B SaaS products. As Founding PM at Delightree, he drove 2× ARR growth and scaled the platform to 150+ franchise brands and 25,000+ users by designing the AI diagnostic intelligence layer that moved the product from compliance alerting to root-cause diagnosis. Prior to Delightree, he owned the identity integration platform at Aquera Labs — a 130+ connector enterprise identity product serving complex enterprise environments — and designed IAM risk scoring products at Accorian.

Siddharth's product focus is the transition from enterprise systems of record and workflow to systems of intelligence: AI-native products that detect risk proactively, diagnose root causes from multi-signal data, and recommend specific actions with enterprise-grade human oversight and auditability.

He publishes a public AI portfolio of ten enterprise B2B projects at github.com/siddharthjaiswal1993-spec.
