## Usage Notes

- Provide 3 references by default; keep references 4 and 5 as backups
- Rotate references based on what the role emphasizes: technical depth, leadership, customer impact, cross-functional execution
- Brief each reference before they are contacted: tell them the company, the role, and 2-3 things you'd like them to speak to
- Follow up with references after the process is complete, whether or not you get the offer
